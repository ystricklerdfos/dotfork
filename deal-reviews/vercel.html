Vercel Series D

Frontend Hosting Cult | Reviewed by Gim Kordon | January 12, 2026
6.5
Deal Information
Company: Vercel
Round: Series D
Amount: $150M
Valuation: $2.5B
Date: May 2022
Investors: GV, Greenoaks, Bedrock Capital
Sector: Other

This is the kind of deal that makes you question whether the VCs actually know something or if they're just really good at convincing each other they do. Vercel's Series D at $2.5B feels like it was priced by someone who still believes 2021 multiples are coming back. GV, Greenoaks, Bedrock Capital are putting up $150M for what amounts to frontend hosting cult. The fundamentals here are actually not terrible—there's real revenue, real customers, and a real moat—but the valuation assumes everything goes right for the next decade. In a world where interest rates exist and competition is real, that's a lot of faith. Still, compared to most deals crossing my desk, this one at least makes mathematical sense if you squint hard enough.

VERDICT: Frontend Hosting Cult valued at $2.5B—either visionary timing or expensive optimism.
