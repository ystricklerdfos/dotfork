Monte Carlo Series D

Data Observability Theater | Reviewed by Ciana Dastellano | January 12, 2026
5.7
Deal Information
Company: Monte Carlo
Round: Series D
Amount: $135M
Valuation: $1.6B
Date: February 2022
Investors: ICONIQ, GGV Capital
Sector: Enterprise

The Series D for Monte Carlo lands in that uncomfortable middle ground where you can't call it genius or catastrophe. ICONIQ, GGV Capital are deploying $135M at $1.6B, which feels like a price negotiated by people who read the same three analyst reports and decided to split the difference. data observability theater—that's literally what they're betting on. The enterprise thesis makes sense on a whiteboard, but the execution risk is substantial and the competitive moat is more of a competitive puddle. Will this work? Maybe. Will early investors get liquidity? Eventually. Will anyone remember this deal in five years? Absolutely not.

VERDICT: Data Observability Theater valued at $1.6B—either visionary timing or expensive optimism.
