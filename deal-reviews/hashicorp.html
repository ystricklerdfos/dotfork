HashiCorp Series E

Infrastructure Nostalgia | Reviewed by Pamy Ahillips | January 12, 2026
5.9
Deal Information
Company: HashiCorp
Round: Series E
Amount: $175M
Valuation: $5.1B
Date: March 2020
Investors: GGV Capital, Redpoint
Sector: Enterprise

The Series E for HashiCorp lands in that uncomfortable middle ground where you can't call it genius or catastrophe. GGV Capital, Redpoint are deploying $175M at $5.1B, which feels like a price negotiated by people who read the same three analyst reports and decided to split the difference. infrastructure nostalgia—that's literally what they're betting on. The enterprise thesis makes sense on a whiteboard, but the execution risk is substantial and the competitive moat is more of a competitive puddle. Will this work? Maybe. Will early investors get liquidity? Eventually. Will anyone remember this deal in five years? Absolutely not.

VERDICT: Infrastructure Nostalgia valued at $5.1B—either visionary timing or expensive optimism.
