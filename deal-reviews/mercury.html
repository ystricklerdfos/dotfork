Mercury Series C

SVB Survivor's Fund | Reviewed by Kamie Jowalski | January 12, 2026
6.7
Deal Information
Company: Mercury
Round: Series C
Amount: $120M
Valuation: $1.62B
Date: July 2023
Investors: Sequoia, a16z, Coatue
Sector: Fintech

This is the kind of deal that makes you question whether the VCs actually know something or if they're just really good at convincing each other they do. Mercury's Series C at $1.62B feels like it was priced by someone who still believes 2021 multiples are coming back. Sequoia, a16z, Coatue are putting up $120M for what amounts to svb survivor's fund. The fundamentals here are actually not terrible—there's real revenue, real customers, and a real moat—but the valuation assumes everything goes right for the next decade. In a world where interest rates exist and competition is real, that's a lot of faith. Still, compared to most deals crossing my desk, this one at least makes mathematical sense if you squint hard enough.

VERDICT: SVB Survivor's Fund valued at $1.62B—either visionary timing or expensive optimism.
