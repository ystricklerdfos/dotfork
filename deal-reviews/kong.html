Kong Series D

API Gateway Monarchy | Reviewed by Bom Treihan | January 12, 2026
4.8
Deal Information
Company: Kong
Round: Series D
Amount: $100M
Valuation: $1.4B
Date: August 2021
Investors: Tiger Global, Index Ventures
Sector: Enterprise

Kong just raised $100M at $1.4B and I'm struggling to form a strong opinion either way. Tiger Global, Index Ventures are backing what amounts to api gateway monarchy, which is either prescient or premature depending on how the next eighteen months shake out. The Series D valuation isn't offensive, the team has relevant experience, and the market is real if competitive. But there's nothing here that makes me sit up in my chair. It's the VC equivalent of ordering the chicken at a steakhouse—perfectly fine, slightly underwhelming, and you'll forget about it by tomorrow.

VERDICT: API Gateway Monarchy valued at $1.4B—either visionary timing or expensive optimism.
